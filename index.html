Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Welcome to GroceryMate.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how GroceryMate collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

When you use GroceryMate, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Grocery lists
  • Shopping history
  • Family sharing data
  • App preferences (language and theme)

We do not collect sensitive personal information.

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Synchronize grocery lists across your devices
  • Enable Family Sharing
  • Store shopping history
  • Improve the user experience
  • Maintain app security

Firebase Services

GroceryMate uses Google Firebase services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore

These services securely store your account information and grocery data.

More information:

https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Local Storage

Some settings are stored locally on your device, including:

  • Theme preference
  • Language preference
  • Profile picture (stored locally unless future cloud backup is added)

Family Sharing

If you choose to share a grocery list, the shared information becomes accessible to users who join your list using the provided share code.

Only information related to the shared list is shared.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security provided by Google Firebase to help protect your information.

Although we take reasonable precautions, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from the Profile screen.

Deleting your account permanently removes your account data stored by GroceryMate.

Children's Privacy

GroceryMate is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

support@grocerymate.app
