Delete Your GroceryMate Account

If you want to delete your GroceryMate account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Open the GroceryMate app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion.

What data is deleted?

  • Your account
  • Your shopping lists
  • Your shopping history
  • Your profile information
  • Your uploaded profile picture

All personal data is permanently deleted immediately after confirmation.
